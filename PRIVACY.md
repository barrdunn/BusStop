# BusStop Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

BusStop does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data you create in the app — folders, items, settings, study
progress — is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.
Nothing is sent to a server. There is no account system, no
analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, and no third-party tracking.

If you choose to export your data as a CSV, the file is created on
your device and shared via the standard iOS share sheet. What you
do with the file after that is your choice.

Notifications are scheduled and delivered locally by iOS. BusStop
does not use Apple Push Notifications or any remote service.
